Lieven Clement is an Associate Professor of Statistical Genomics at Ghent University, where he leads the statOmics Group. He is an expert in developing statistical methods and open source tools for differential omics data analysis. His research efforts resulted in numerous publications on novel methods and tools for, and applied research in omics data analysis. 

His lab is built around three strategic research pillars each connected to an omics domain: metagenomics, transcriptomics and proteomics and he also has a strong interest in leveraging his expertise to translational research.

Affiliation: Ghent University, Ghent, BE
